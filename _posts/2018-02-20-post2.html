Gotchya #1

 Out of the box, the rendering engine (is it liquid?) will not render pages that are date stamped at a future time.  Spent 20 minutes trying to figure out why my page wasnt generating.  =(

Gotchya #2

The date prepended in the post file name (yyyy-mm-dd-you-post-name) does not matter at all.  In fact, the title also does not matter.  The values in the "front matter" portion of the posts is what is used to determine if a post will be generated for the site.
