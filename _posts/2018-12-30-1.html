I noted that the following sites are no longer being updated due to the current shutdown-

  • https://www.usa.gov/ - innocuously named, but primarily provides information about the US government

Their full mission statement can be found here

"We create and organize timely, needed government information and services and make them accessible to the public anytime, anywhere, via their channel of choice."

  • National Park Service - availability of parks and protection of wildlife are probably one of the lower concerns

However, some websites are more equal than others and are operating normally. 

  • https://www.irs.gov/ - tax collection
  • https://www.army.mil/ - military
  • https://www.whitehouse.gov/ - propaganda
  • https://www.cia.gov/ - intelligence


A full list of government sites is available here

https://www.usa.gov/federal-agencies/
