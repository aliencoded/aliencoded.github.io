I can do it.  I can write!  I have so many great ideas to share and people will want to read what I write.  That is what I told myself when I started to write for a blog.  But when the time came to actually put ink on paper, or in this case type text between <p>s, the task was much more daunting, and doubt creeps in.  Who would want to read what I write on this corner of the internet.  There is a whole internet out there for people to read!  Reddit, Wikipedia, Facebook, Twitter.  Who has time for traditional blog reading nowadays anyway.  It, especially, will not be people listening to podcasts or those endlessly watching cat videos on loop on YouTube(I think I already use that expression too much).  But, alas, I must remind myself- the blog created here is not particularly meant to read to be consumed by a large unknown, uncaring, and usually quite critical, crowd.  Rather, it is nothing more than an exercise in my creative ability in the hopes that it will leave me feeling fulfilled. 

But that cannot be the only goal that is achieved.  If my ideas were to resonate with some reader, perhaps then they may nod their heads in agreement and perhaps lead to a certain sort of epiphany for them.  In any case, this blog can hopefully function as a public journal to semi stream-of-consciousness sort of blabbering.  There isn't even a spell check that I am using right now!
